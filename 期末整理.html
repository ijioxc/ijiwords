Grammar — 文法表

-ing Form vs Infinitive 動名詞 vs 不定詞

以下 6 個動詞接 doing 與 to do 意思完全不同，需特別記憶。

1. remember
remember doing
記得曾做過某事（回憶）
I remember meeting her before.
remember to do
記得要去做某事（提醒）
Remember to lock the door.
2. forget
forget doing
忘記曾做過（事已做）
I'll never forget seeing that film.
forget to do
忘記要做（事未做）
I forgot to buy tickets.
3. stop
stop doing
停止做某事（結束）
He stopped driving because he was tired.
stop to do
停下來去做另一件事
He stopped to drink some coffee.
4. go on
go on doing
繼續做同一件事
Billy went on practicing every day.
go on to do
接著做下一件事
Billy went on to become a famous dancer.
5. try
try doing
嘗試做（實驗看看）
Try taking the aspirin.
try to do
努力嘗試做（可能很難）
He tried to recall her name.
6. regret
regret doing
後悔做了某事
I regret not studying harder.
regret to do
遺憾地做某事（正式）
We regret to inform you the concert has been cancelled.
Past Modals of Deduction 過去式情態動詞推測

公式：modal + have + p.p.（過去分詞）

句型 確信度 中文意思 例句
must have + p.p. 確定（+） 一定有…（正面推測） It must have been stolen when I was looking at the picture.
can't / couldn't have + p.p. 確定（−） 不可能有…（否定推測） She couldn't have taken it — she was working with me.
might / could have + p.p. 不確定 可能有…（不確定） He might have hidden it in his briefcase.
should have + p.p. 應該但沒有 本應…但沒有（責備） I should have been more careful.

推測強度排序

must have › might / could have › can't have

被動式：must / might / can't + have been + p.p.

Listening — 聽力題整理

Unit 8 · S8.5 Jim & Liz — 借錢還錢 Money
情境：Jim 上週向 Liz 借了 $10。Liz 提出「每週還一點點」的分期方式，Jim 認為這聽起來合理。
QLiz owes Jim a small amount of money. True or False?
AFalse — Jim owes Liz $10 (not the other way around).
QShe doesn't always keep her promises. T or F?
AFalse — She does always keep her promises.
QJim wants her to pay a set amount each week. T or F?
ATrue.
Unit 8 · S8.8 美國打招呼文化 — Names & Greetings Social Customs
情境：主持人介紹美國人見面的習慣 — 通常在正式場合仍用 Mr./Ms. + 姓，但大多數人很快就改用名字（first name）。握手是常見的問候方式。
重點美國人在商業場合會先用 Mr./Ms. + last name，但通常很快就換成 first name。
重點Hugging 在朋友之間很常見，但在職場初次見面不建議。
重點Being late（遲到）在美國被視為不禮貌，尤其商業場合。
重點Body language 因文化差異很大，如手勢「OK」在某些國家意思不同。
Unit 9 · S9.2 Lisa & Jeff — 咖啡廳扒竊事件 Crime / Theft
情境：Lisa 在咖啡廳坐著，一對年輕情侶靠近拍照，趁機把她的皮包（含信用卡、手機）偷走。Lisa 去銀行取消信用卡。
QWhere was Lisa when it happened?
AShe was sitting in a café.
QHow did the thieves distract her?
AThey looked like tourists and asked her to take a picture of them.
QWhat did Lisa do after she realized her purse was gone?
AShe checked her seat and table, then called the bank to cancel her credit card.
QThe man in the jewelry store was posing as…
AA fake banker / someone from a financial company.
Conversation 2 Dan & Ingrid — 珠寶店詐騙 Scam
情境：Ingrid 幾年前在珠寶店工作時，一個陌生男子帶著項鍊說「這條可能值 $600」，Ingrid 被轉移注意力，後來發現男子的同夥其實是在用項鍊換取現金。騙局損失約 $600，Ingrid 後來辭職。
QWhere did the scam take place?
AIn a jewelry store.
QHow much money was involved?
AAbout $600.
QWhy did Ingrid quit her job?
ABecause the salary was only $600/month — not worth it after the incident.
QWhat kind of scam technique was used?
ADistraction — one person draws attention while the other takes action.
